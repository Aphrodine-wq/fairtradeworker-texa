## üè† Homeowner Fees

### Platform Fee: $20 Flat

- Applied once per completed job
- Covers payment processing, escrow protection, dispute resolution
- No hidden fees or percentages
- Same price regardless of job size

### Extended Draw Fee (Large Projects Only)

For projects with milestone-based payment schedules:

| Project Size | Fee |
|--------------|-----|
| Under $5,000 | No draw fee (platform fee only) |
| $5,000 - $25,000 | 3% of total |
| $25,000 - $100,000 | 2.75% of total |
| Over $100,000 | 2.5% of total |

> **Note**: Draw fees only apply to projects using the extended draw schedule feature, not standard one-time payments.

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Why $59/month for Pro?

$59/month is positioned to be:

- **Accessible**: Less than 1 hour of billable work for most trades
- **Competitive**: 70-90% cheaper than lead-gen alternatives
- **Sustainable**: Allows us to provide quality service without selling data

### Are there any hidden fees?

No. The prices listed above are the complete pricing structure:

- Pro: $59/month flat
- Platform fee: $20 flat per job
- Extended draw: 2.5-3% only for large projects with draw schedules

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro subscriptions can be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fee. You retain Pro features until the end of your billing period.

### Do homeowners pay the platform fee or contractors?

Homeowners pay the $20 platform fee. Contractors keep 100% of their quoted price.
